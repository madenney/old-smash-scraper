RANK TOURNAMENT**

Now that we have around 180+ members in the Smash Zelda - Hyrule&#x27;s Wise Princess Facebook group, I think it’s time to see which Zelda stands on top. This tournament will be in a straight-bracket style. For those of you who do not know what this is, this is where every player starts off in a winners bracket and if they lose they get sent to the losers bracket, if they lose once more in the losers bracket, they are out. Matches will best of 3 with winners &amp; losers semis, winners &amp; losers finals, and grand finals being best of 5. 2 stocked matches, time will be 6 minutes per match. Items set to off, customs set to off. Miis are allowed their full moveset.

We will be following the recommended ruleset: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1CFaeel_cVIImEZ7ZrIrb_fSva5H3tsN24yp2INyrVLE/edit

Legal stages:
* Battlefield*
* Final Destination**
* Smashville
* Lylat Cruise
* Town and City

Counterpicks:
* Omega Stages** (Linked with FD)
* Dreamland* (Linked with Battlefield)

Players may agree to play on any particular stage if they want to.

Modified Daves Stupid Rule: A player may not choose the stage they previously won on most recently unless this stage was selected by the other player.

Process goes as follow:

Round 1:
- Both players select Zelda.
- Player A chooses one stage from the legal list to strike, removing it from possible choices for only Round 1.
- Player B chooses two stages from the legal list to strike, removing it from possible choices for only Round 1.
- Player A chooses from the remaining two stages to start the match.

Round 2:
- Winner strikes one stage from both legal and counterpicks, removing it from possible choices for Round 2.
- Loser chooses stage &amp; has option to switch to another character besides Zelda.
- Winner must choose Zelda.

Round 3 - 5:
- Repeat Round 2’s steps until one player has 2 wins (or 3 wins for semis and finals).

In the event where a match’s time ends. The player with the most stocks win. If stocks are even, the player with the least percentage wins. If the percentages are even. The players are to rematch with just one stock and 3 minutes. In the unlikely event that another percentage tie happens, a draw is awarded to both players. Draws are worth 0 points.

Report your wins to the tournament organizer or admins if smash.gg is not working for you.

All sets are best of 3.

Stage List:
Starter Stages:
Battlefield*
Final Destination**
Lylat Cruise
Smashville
Town &amp; City

Counterpick Stages:
Dream Land 64*
Omega Stages**

Banning stages with one * asterisk * also bans the same stage in the counter pick section. Banning a stage with two ** asterisks ** also bans all other stages with two asterisks.

Additional Rules:
Players select their characters, then stage strike (each player strikes out a single stage) or may agree upon the first stage. Winner of game 1 bans one stage, loser picks stage. Winner then selects character, and then loser selects character. Repeat this process until all matches are played.

DSR: Players may not select the stage they have previously won on unless agreed upon by both parties.

Stalling: Any player intentially delaying the game or breaking the game by exploiting bugs will result in an automatic forfeiture from the tournament.

Pausing: If a player pauses the game, that player must lose 1 stock. Pausing functionality should be disabled prior to the beginning of the match.

Warm ups/Hand warmers: Should not take excessive amounts of time, up to 30 seconds maximum.

Timeout: If the time runs out on the clock the winner is the player with the most stocks, if both players are tied for stocks the winner is the player with the least percentage taken, if both players are tied for percent, the winner is the player with the least damage taken on both stocks, if both players are tied still refer to self-destruct/double K.O.

Self-Destruct/Double K.O.: If a player uses any move that results in a self destruct, whoever appears on the winning screen is the winner of the match. If both players are sent into Sudden Death, than a second 3 minute 1 stock match will be played to decide the winner. Repeat this process until a winner is decided unless a specific move is repeatedly triggering the event.
